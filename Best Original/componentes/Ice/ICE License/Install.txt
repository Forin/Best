ICE License is installed on your computer, you just need to launch Delphi and install the component.

Go to ICE License folder, and select your Delphi version, after double-click on "ICEv2_D6.dpk" for Delphi 6 and compile.

Don't forget to add ICE License folder on your Delphi IDE environnement folder (go to "Tools", "Libraries", add ICE License folder)

After you can see ICE License VCL in your VCL palette.
